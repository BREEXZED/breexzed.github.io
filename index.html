BREEXZED

Trapped within constraints that suffocates being and becoming, i untie myself from the shackles binded on me, through the dispersion of my soul and the traces i leave of having existed, this site serves as a home for the one in me who knows nothing, but wants to learn.

Having seen how i saw myself, i realized, there was little i knew apart from myself. Self-knowledge is no mystery, understanding one thing is an implicit attribution to the one who understands. Signal

Signals and trails.

Here, I document the signals I encounter in the world as they express themselves within my field of resonance. I note them explicitly, along with the traces of how I encountered them. I explore their diverse natures to learn from them and to make predictions inferred from the lenses they share. Signals may include, but are not limited to, a person, a niche, or a group of people within a culture or subcultural organism.
